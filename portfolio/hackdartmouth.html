HACKDARTMOUTH


HackDartmouth 2015 was Dartmouth's first ever Hackathon -- we brought in over 200 participants and had over 500 applicants. For the Hackathon, I was responsible for creating all marketing collateral and branding to spearhead recruitment. I decided to go for a relatively simplistic look that capitalizes on geometric shapes and clean icons.

POSTERS

We chose the slogan "Design, Develop, Discover" to relay how HackDartmouth was meant to appeal to a variety of people. We wanted to reach designers, developers, and also entrepreneurs to get an eclectic mix of participants.
