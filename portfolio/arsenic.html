ARSENIC


Arsenic aims to raise awareness about the dangers of arsenic. For this project, we worked with researchers at Dartmouth Hitchcock Medical Center and the Geisel School of Medicine. We mainly wanted to target pregnant women and new mothers, as arsenic has many negative effects on infants and young children. To inform people, we created a storybook-like scroll site, along with animated effects, which shows how arsenic gets into food.

The site purposefully includes cartoon-like graphics to avoid scares - we wanted to make sure our viewers knew about the dangers of arsenic, but also felt empowered afterwards to reduce arsenic by following simple tips.

In this project, I contributed to both the ideation and creation phases. After brainstorming and settling on our idea, I worked on custom graphics and icons for all of the parallax site.
